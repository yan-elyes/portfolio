Fraud Alerts

Tech Stack: Python, Machine learning, Deep neural learning, NLP


    Technical used:
  • Machine Learning:

    AI can use machine learning algorithms to analyze large amounts of data and detect fraudulent patterns or behavior. This can include techniques such as decision trees, neural networks, classification algorithms and unsupervised learning methods.

  • NLP:

    Fraud can involve fraudulent communications or documents. AI using NLP can analyze this textual data for clues to fraud or deception.

  • Deep Neural Networks:

    These multi-layered artificial neural networks can be used to analyze large amounts of structured and unstructured data, identifying patterns or anomalies that may indicate fraudulent activity.

      • Purpose:

    - Automate a process that is a source of human error.
    - Reduce false positives.
    - Avoid convictions and heavy fines.
